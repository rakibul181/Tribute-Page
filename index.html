Jamal Nazrul Islam

One of Gratest Bangladeshi mathematical physicist and cosmologist


Here's a time line Jamal Nazrul's life:

born

  • 1939-born on 24 February 1939 in Jhenaidah, East Bengal.

Education

  • 1957 - received a BSc degree from St. Xavier's College at the University of Calcutta.

  • 1959 - got his Honors in Functional Mathematics and Theoretical Physics from Cambridge University

  • 1960 - completed his Masters in 1960. As a student of the Trinity College

  • 1968 - obtained his PhD in applied mathematics and theoretical physics from Trinity College, Cambridge

  • 1982 - Doctor of Science for University of Cambridge

Academic career

  • 1963-1965 - pre Doctoral Fello at University Of MaryLand

  • 1967-1971 - worked in the Institute of Theoretical Astronomy (later amalgamated to Institute of Astronomy, Cambridge)

  • 1971-1972 - worked as a researcher in California Institute of Technology and University of Washington

  • 1973-1974 - served as the faculty of Applied Mathematics of King's College London

  • 1975-1978 - Scientific Research at University of Cardiff

  • 1978-1984 - joined the faculty of City University London

  • 1984-2006 - Professor at University of Chittagong

  • 2006-2013 - Professor Emeritus at the University of Chittagong
